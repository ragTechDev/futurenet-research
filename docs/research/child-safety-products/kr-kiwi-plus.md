# Kiwi Plus
[KIWI PLUS](https://www.kiwiplus.co.kr/) is a world class innovative venture firm with a unique combination of strengths in both software development and device manufacturing.

## Vision
Globally leading brand for Childcare Devices:

Protect children from all kinds of risks through creating a world where children are excited and parents are free from the constant need to be concerned for their kids.

## Mission
To lead the market in Context-Aware Networks, enabling mutual understanding between humans, products, and services.

## Product
Provide optimized products and services for our target markets, and develop platform-based characters and content.

## Technology
An integrated technology cloud of miniature hardware modules and software, big data technology, and connectivity including 3G, LTE-M, Beacon, and LBS

## Kids Products

### U+Kids phone MOONO Edition 2
![U+Kids phone MOONO Edition 2](https://www.kiwiplus.co.kr/img/moono2/pc/pc_product_moonoedition2_img.png)
![U+Kids phone MOONO Edition 2 Theme](https://www.kiwiplus.co.kr/img/moono2/pc/pc_product_img_moonoedition2_theme04.png)

The [U+Kids phone MOONO Edition 2](https://www.kiwiplus.co.kr/kidsphonemoonoedition2) is a child-friendly smartphone, using the Samsung Galaxy A17 as the base model, that is designed to be used by children. It is a great way for children to stay connected with their family and friends while also being able to use the internet for educational purposes.

- Various Character Themes in the U+Kids phone MOONO Edition 2
- AI Safe Care+ to protects kids; KiwiPlay+ Service: Pair the Guardian app with the kids’ app to care for their smartphone use. With ‘AI Harmful Content Detection’ and ‘Kids’ Health Information’, enjoy the safer KiwiPlay+ experience.
- Location Tracking & Safe Area Management: Check your child’s movement history, and receive notifications for home, school, and safe zone arrivals and departures.
- Remote Monitoring & Control: Remotely take photos using your child’s phone camera to check their surroundings, change the ringer mode, or make an instant call.
- Check Kids’ Location Alerts: When your child sends their current location using the 'Share My Location' widget, you can view it instantly in the Guardian app.
- Smartphone Usage Time Management: Manage your child’s daily screen time and app usage, or instantly block internet and apps when needed.
- Remaining Usage Time Alerts: When the limited usage time is approaching, the child receives a notification of the remaining time.
- App Usage Time Monitoring: Monitor when and how long your child uses apps, and compare their usage with peers.
- AI Analysis Report: View AI reports that provide guidance on appropriate screen time for your child.
- AI Detection of Harmful Messages & Images: AI detects suspicious harmful messages or images on your child’s smartphone and provides notifications to guardians.
- Blocking Harmful Content: Manage allowed contacts for calls and messages, block harmful apps or websites, and restrict access to unsafe content.
- Kids’ Health Information: Helpful health, medical, wellness, and policy news that supports your kids’ well-being is regularly updated.
- Kids’ Growth Diary: Track your kids’ growth progress, compare with peers through trend graphs, and view AI analysis reports.
- My Notice Board: View today’s schedule, to-dos, praise stickers, and habit building activities on the Kid's app home.
- Praise Stickers: Kids can collect praise stickers by keeping the promises they make with their guardians.
- Snap! Habit Builder: Kids set their own habit goals and take daily photo check-ins, and receive encouragement and rewards from parents.
- Collecting Fish-Shaped Buns: When kids complete today’s challenge successfully, they earn a fish-shaped bun as a reward.
- Using Fish-shaped Buns: Children can exchange the Fish-shaped Buns they earn for Free Mode coupons, smartphone themes, or items to decorate Moono’s room.

### U+Kids phone CHOONSIK2
![U+Kids phone CHOONSIK2](https://www.kiwiplus.co.kr/img/choonsik2/pc/pc_product_img_kakaolittlefriendsphone7_main.png)

Samsung Galazy A24 Smartphone; licensed by KAKAO.

- Safe care to protect children + KiwiPlay+ kids
- Location notification widget: Kids can send their current location and a photo of the location to their parents
- Check your kid's location and manage safe areas: You can check your kid's real-time location and travel route, and be notified when they leave home, school or other designated safe areas
- App usage monitoring: You can check your kid's smartphone usage on a daily and weekly basis.
- Smartphone usage in hour: You can check your kid's smartphone usage at any point and time of the day.
- Floating notification: When kids use their smartphones, floating notifications will guide them on their remaining usage time.
- My Notifications: When kids click the My Notification menu, they are guided on what to do next through CHOONSIK's comments.
- My Emotions: When kids set their emotions, CHOONSIK will leave a comment according to the set emotion.
- My Sweet Potatoes: Once kids sucessfully complete today's challenge, they can receive a sweet potato.
- Using my Sweet Potatoes: Sweet potatoes collected by kids can be exchanged for gifts. Gifts include modified speech bubbles and backgrounds (which can be applied from the room decoration screen) as well as 'Free Mode' coupons.
- My Memories: Kids can use photos and videos to record what they did today. If kids upload a memory as a 'shared memory', parents are able to see the posts and like them. If kids upload a memory as a 'private memory', only they can see it.
- Click!: Developing a habit; Kids can set their own goals, challenges,
and form habits by themselves.
