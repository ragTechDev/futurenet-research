# ğŸ™ During the Interview

### **Opening Script**
Introduce:
- who we are
- what FutureNet aims to explore
- why their input matters
- consent

### **Conversation Approach**
- Ask open-ended questions
- Listen more than you speak
- Avoid leading questions
- Follow curiosity

### **Note-taking**
Capture:
- Direct quotes (word-for-word where possible)
- Pain points & emotions
- Needs & expectations
- Surprising insights

Use the template:  
[`/docs/research/stakeholders/interview-notes-template.md`](docs\research\stakeholders\interview-notes-template.md)

Use tags such as `#safety #communication #screenaddiction #schooluse #trust #privacy` for easy searching across repository.
