# Consent & Privacy Policy (Research Interviews)

We respect your privacy. Participation is voluntary and you may discontinue anytime.

### Data Handling
- Notes and recordings (if permitted) are stored securely within a private research folder.
- Only the FutureNet design and research team may access the recordings.
- Personal identifiers will be removed in published summaries.

### Usage
Your feedback will be used to shape UI/UX, feature decisions, and safety design for FutureNet.

By participating, you consent to:
- Internal research usage only
- Anonymous insights being shared publicly in project docs

You may request deletion of your data at any time.
