## Research Strategy Recommendations

### Search Terms for Literature Review

**Product R&D Keywords:**
- Internet/online/cyber safety
- Digital media literacy/wellness
- Social media, online gaming, screen time, parental controls
- Harmful content: sexual/violent/suicide/self-harm/cyberbullying

**Target User Keywords:**
- Childhood, adolescents, preschool, children, teen, youths, young adults, students

**Developmental Research Keywords:**
- Emotional/cognitive/psychological development/regulation
- Obesity, sleep, neurodevelopment, addiction

## Specific Resources to Explore

### IMDA Online Safety Framework
- **Online Safety Act** - Legal requirements
- **Codes of Practice for Online Safety** - Social media service requirements for child safety
- **Online Safety Report** - Includes Mystery Shoppers Test (potential automation opportunity)

### Singapore Standards Council
- Free access via NLB Multimedia Stations
- Industry best practices (guidelines, not requirements)
- Access: Login with library account → e-resources icon

### MOH Resources
- Guidance on screen use in children
- Evidence review consolidating scientific research
- Screen use expert subgroup (potential expert consultation)

### ProQuest Central
- Business reports and trade publications
- Peer-reviewed journals
- Systematic review articles
- Access: [eresources.nlb.gov.sg](https://eresources.nlb.gov.sg)
