# NLB Launch Pad Information

## Example of online safety ratings
1. Online Safety Assessment Report 2024
https://www.imda.gov.sg/-/media/imda/files/regulations-and-licensing/regulations/online-safety/online-safety-assessment-report-2024-designated-social-media-services.pdf
IMDA assesses the effectiveness of Designated Social Media Services (SMSs) in complying with the Code of Practice for Online Safety. Each Designated SMS received an overall rating and individual ratings for four sections including their safety measures for children.
Pages 10-11: Lists the methodology used in assessing and rating the Designated SMSs.
Resources on children development across different age groups

2. Families for Life Parenting - Families for Life (FfL)
https://familiesforlife.sg/pages/fflp
FFL is a council under the Ministry of Social and Family Development which provides evidence-based resources for parents in raising children aged up to 19 years old. This includes articles on child development and online safety for different age groups.
