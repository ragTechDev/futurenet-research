# Interview Guide – Stakeholder: [Parent / Teacher / Student / Expert]

## Opening Script
Thank you for taking the time to speak with us. We’re exploring how to create a safer digital environment and communication device for primary school–aged children. Your perspective will help shape our early decisions. There are no right or wrong answers; we’re here to learn from your experiences.

## Consent
- May we record this call for internal research use only?
- Your identity will not be linked to the data unless you request otherwise.

---

## Warm-up Questions
1. Could you tell us a little about your experience with children and digital devices?
2. What role does technology currently play in your daily routines?

---

## Core Questions
### **Current Behaviors**
- What devices do your children use, and for what activities?
- How much time do they spend on them?

### **Concerns & Pain Points**
- What worries you most about kids on the internet?
- What challenges have you faced when managing usage?

### **Feature Expectations**
- If a controlled, kid-friendly phone existed, what must it include?
- What would make you trust or distrust the platform?

### **Environment & Context**
- How does your school/community approach technology use?
- What communication needs are most important?

### **Success**
- How would you know this solution is working well?

---

## Closing
- Is there anything important we didn’t ask?
- Would you be open to future testing or feedback?

**Thank you for your time.**
